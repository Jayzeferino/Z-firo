# PRODUCT-CONTEXT.md Format

This document is the single source of truth for the product's marketing positioning, audience personas, pain points, and terminology. It should be updated inline as the marketer/user refines the product vision.

## Structure

```markdown
# [Product Name]

**Tagline**: [One-sentence tagline summarizing the primary transformation or outcome]
**Description**: [One-paragraph description of the product, what it does, and why it exists]

## Target Personas
Define 1-2 primary target personas.

### Persona 1: [Name/Archetype]
- **Role/Demographics**: [e.g., Tech-savvy Solopreneur, Head of Sales at B2B SaaS]
- **Core Goals**: [What are they trying to achieve?]
- **Current Workflow**: [How do they solve this problem today?]

## Core Problems & Pain Points
List the specific frustrations the target audience experiences that this product solves.

- **[Pain Point 1]**: [Detailed explanation of the frustration and its impact]
- **[Pain Point 2]**: [e.g., Waste of time, manual errors, lack of visibility]
- **[Pain Point 3]**: [e.g., High cost of alternatives]

## Core Features & Benefits
Map product capabilities directly to customer outcomes.

| Feature | Technical Description (What it is) | Benefit / Transformation (What it means for them) |
|---------|------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| **[Feature 1]** | [e.g., Drag-and-drop builder] | [e.g., Build landing pages in 5 minutes without writing code] |
| **[Feature 2]** | [e.g., Real-time analytics dashboard] | [e.g., Instant visibility into conversion rates to make quick decisions] |

## Positioning & Competitors
- **Key Differentiator**: [Why do customers choose this product over alternatives?]
- **Primary Alternatives**: [List main competitors or current workarounds (e.g. Excel sheets)]
- **Value Proposition**: [The main promise of value to be delivered]

## Vocabulary & Style Rules
Define the ubiquitous language for all subsequent marketing materials (copywriting, landing pages, emails).

- **[Term 1]**:
  [Description of the term and how it should be framed]
  _Avoid_: [List of words or phrases to avoid using in copy]

- **[Term 2]**:
  [Description of the term]
  _Avoid_: [Alternative terms that sound clinical or misaligned]
```

## Guidelines for Creation & Updates

- **Keep it focused**: Only include facts and decisions that directly impact customer communication and marketing angles.
- **Update inline**: The moment a term is clarified or a benefit is refined during conversation, edit `PRODUCT-CONTEXT.md` immediately. Do not defer updates.
- **No placeholders**: Always fill in real details derived from the user interview.
